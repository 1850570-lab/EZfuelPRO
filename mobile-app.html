Roadside dispatch

Built for the driver, not the dispatcher

What it does

Cheapest-stop map

In-network stations along the route, sorted by your effective post-discount price and accounting for deadhead miles.

Issue MoneyCodes

Generate one-time codes for lumper fees, scale tickets, parking, or cash advances — straight from the app, no portal trip needed.

Live balance & activity

See available balance, pending authorizations, and the last 30 days of fills — searchable by location and date.

Freeze the card

Two taps if the card's lost or you spot something weird. Unfreeze the same way once it's resolved.

One-tap roadside

SOS button at the bottom of every screen. Connects to a real human dispatcher with your location and truck info pre-loaded.

Receipt capture

Snap a photo of paper receipts (lumpers, weigh stations, repairs) and they attach to the right transaction automatically.

Smart alerts

Pings when you're approaching a discount-friendly station, when a card is declined, or when dispatch needs a confirmation.

In-app chat

Quick questions to support without leaving the app. Voice messages supported for the times typing isn't realistic.

Night mode by default

Dark from the moment you open it. Big tap targets. Designed for someone wearing gloves at 2 a.m. in a sleeper.
