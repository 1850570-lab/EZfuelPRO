Frequently asked questions

How is the per-gallon discount calculated?
Your discount is based on the number of active trucks on your account. The more trucks you run, the higher your per-gallon savings — starting at 12¢/gallon for 1–5 trucks and going up to 60¢/gallon for fleets of 50+ trucks. The discount is applied at the point of sale, so your drivers see the savings on the receipt immediately.
Are there any hidden fees or transaction charges?
No. We charge a flat weekly billing cycle with no per-transaction fees, no monthly minimums, and no activation charges. What you see in the discount schedule is what you get.
What ELD brands does your tech support cover?
We're provider-agnostic. Our team supports KeepTruckin (Motive), Samsara, ELD Mandate, Garmin, Platform Science, and most other major ELD devices. If you're switching ELD providers, we'll handle the install and data migration too.
Can you help with dashcam setup?
Yes — we configure and troubleshoot dashcam systems from Samsara, Lytx, SmartDrive, Motive, and others. We'll handle placement guidance, network connectivity, cloud storage setup, and driver coaching configuration.
How fast can I get cards for my drivers?
Most applications are approved same-day. Physical cards ship overnight, and we can issue virtual card numbers immediately so your drivers can start fueling the same day you sign up.
Is tech support included with fuel cards or is it extra?
Basic tech support (phone and email help during business hours, plus IFTA report generation) is included free with every fuel card account. For 24/7 coverage, priority response, on-site setup, and dedicated account management, we offer a Premium support tier — see our Pricing page for details.
