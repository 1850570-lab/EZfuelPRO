How the estimate works

Tier mapping

Truck count maps to your fleet tier — Starter (1–5), Growth (6–20), Pro (21–50), or Enterprise (50+). Each tier has a discount range we've actually delivered.

Account type

Credit accounts unlock the upper end of each tier's discount range, in exchange for underwriting. Prepaid is instant and lands at the standard end.

Volume

Annual savings = weekly gallons × 52 × discount range. We round to the nearest hundred so the number isn't fake-precise.

What's not in here

Time savings, fewer disputed transactions, and roadside cost-avoidance from our dispatch are real but harder to quantify. They're additional upside.
