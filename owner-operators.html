Built for solo drivers

Three things that matter when it's just you

Same-day Prepaid approval

No credit check. Fund the account with ACH, debit, or even your business credit card. Cards activate the same day you apply — virtual numbers in your driver app for the first fuel-up before the plastic ships.

MoneyCodes when the swipe fails

Lumper fees, scale tickets, parking, a tow that doesn't take cards — issue a one-time code from the app and the cashier just types it in. No more truck-stop ATM fees, no calling someone to wire cash.

Roadside dispatch you actually use

Tire blow on I-40 at 2 a.m. is the same problem whether you're 1 truck or 100. One number, real human in under 60 seconds, and a tech dispatched with a quoted price before they roll. No "we'll call you back."
