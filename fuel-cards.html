Complete control

Built-in card management features

Set restrictions, monitor spend, and lock down fraud in real time. Every card is a tool, not just a line item.

Per-Driver Spending Limits

Cap daily or weekly spend per card. Prevent runaway fuel costs and catch unusual activity instantly.

Fuel-Only Restrictions

Lock cards to fuel purchases only. Prevent drivers from buying snacks, merchandise, or anything else at the pump.

Location-Based Controls

Define geofences. Cards only work at approved locations — keep regional drivers regional.

Instant Card Freeze/Unfreeze

Lost card? Suspected fraud? Freeze it in seconds from the app — no phone calls, no delays. Unfreeze when you're ready.

Real-Time Transaction Alerts

Get notifications for every transaction. Know the second a card is swiped, where, and for how much.

Virtual Card Numbers

Generate one-time or recurring virtual cards for same-day fueling. No physical card needed — drivers start saving immediately.

MoneyCode Issuance

Issue one-time codes from the portal for lumper fees, scale tickets, repairs, or cash advances. Send to any driver in seconds — works at every truck stop, no card swipe required.

Flexible Funding

Top up your account with ACH, wire, debit, or even a credit card — Visa, Mastercard, Amex. Run fuel through your business card and stack the rewards on top of your tier discount.

Driver Mobile App

Drivers see the cheapest in-network stations along their route, check card balance, view recent transactions, and reach support — all from their phone. iOS and Android.
