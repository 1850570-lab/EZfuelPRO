Who we are

Built by fleet people,
for fleet people.

We started as frustrated operators sick of opaque fuel card pricing and calling three different vendors. Now we're a single point of contact for fuel savings and roadside support.

Our story

From frustration to solution

EZ FUEL was born in a truck cab. Our founders were running a 35-truck operation when fuel cards, ELDs, dashcams, and IFTA reporting all started breaking down at the same time. Three vendor calls, two emergency meetings, and zero real help later, they realized the industry was stuck in the 2000s.

So they built what they needed: a fuel card company that actually showed discounts upfront, paired with tech support staffed by people who understand the road.

Today, EZ FUEL partners with 2,400+ fleets. We've saved our customers over $50 million at the pump and handled hundreds of thousands of tech support cases. And we're just getting started.
